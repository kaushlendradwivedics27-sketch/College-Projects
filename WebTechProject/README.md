🌾 Smart Surplus Food Redistribution Platform
📌 Project Overview

The Smart Surplus Food Redistribution Platform is a web-based application designed to reduce food waste and eliminate hunger by connecting food donors with NGOs and volunteers.

The platform enables restaurants, event organizers, hostels, and individuals to donate surplus food, while NGOs can view and accept available food donations nearby. The system ensures timely distribution before food expiry and tracks overall impact.

🎯 Objectives

Reduce food waste

Support underprivileged communitiess

Improve coordination between donors and NGOs

🔄 System Workflow

Donor adds surplus food.

Food appears as "Available".

NGO views nearby food.

NGO accepts donation.

Food is delivered before expiry.

Admin dashboard updates impact statistics.

📊 Smart Matching Logic (Concept)

The platform prioritizes food distribution based on:

Expiry time (urgent food first)

Location proximity

Priority Score Formula (Concept):

Priority Score = Expiry Time Remaining + Distance Factor

Lower score = Higher priority.
